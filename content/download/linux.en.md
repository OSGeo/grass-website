## Installation

Install the `grass` package on your Linux distribution. Have a look at
[Repology](https://repology.org/project/grass-gis/versions) for an extended
list of GRASS packages or directly check the
[packaging status](https://repology.org/badge/vertical-allrepos/grass-gis.svg?exclude_unsupported=1&exclude_sources=modules,site&columns=3)
to quickly know which GRASS version is currently available for your distro.
